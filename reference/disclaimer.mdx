## Financial risk

Trading cryptocurrency futures carries a high degree of risk and may not be suitable for all participants. Key risk factors include:

- **Leverage amplifies losses.** A position using 10x leverage can lose its entire value on a 10% adverse move. At 20x, a 5% move is sufficient for full liquidation.
- **Market volatility.** Cryptocurrency markets operate 24/7 and can experience extreme price swings within seconds, including gaps that bypass stop-loss orders.
- **Liquidation risk.** If a leveraged position moves against you and your margin is exhausted, the exchange will forcibly close the position, resulting in total loss of the margin deposited.
- **Software risk.** Bugs, network failures, API outages, or misconfiguration can cause the bot to behave unexpectedly — opening, failing to close, or duplicating positions.
- **Exchange risk.** Exchange insolvency, withdrawal freezes, or API changes can affect your ability to access funds or execute orders.

You should only participate in futures trading if you fully understand these risks and can afford to lose the funds you deploy.

## Responsible trading practices

If you choose to use this software with real funds, follow these practices to limit your risk:

<Tip>
  Start on testnet with paper trading before deploying real capital. Set `"testnet": true` in `config.json` to use Binance or Bybit testnet endpoints at no cost.
</Tip>

- **Use low leverage to start.** Begin at 2x–5x while you validate strategy behavior in live market conditions.
- **Set stop-losses on every trade.** Configure `stop_loss_pct` and `max_daily_loss_pct` in the `risk_management` block of `config.json`.
- **Limit position size.** Keep `max_position_pct` small (e.g. 2–5%) so no single trade can cause disproportionate damage to your account.
- **Monitor the bot actively.** Automated systems can malfunction. Do not leave the bot running unattended for extended periods without checking its status.
- **Never risk funds you cannot afford to lose.** Only deploy capital that, if lost entirely, would not affect your financial stability or wellbeing.

```json
{
  "leverage": 3,
  "max_position_pct": 2.0,
  "trailing_stop_pct": 1.5,
  "risk_management": {
    "max_open_positions": 3,
    "max_daily_loss_pct": 2.0,
    "stop_loss_pct": 1.5,
    "take_profit_pct": 3.0
  }
}
```
